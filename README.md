# SpoolTags

A 3D printer filament tracking app for iOS, iPadOS, and macOS.

## About

SpoolTags helps you track every 3D printer spool with QR codes and NFC tags. Know exactly what you have, how much is left, and when you're running low.

**Website:** [spooltags.app](https://spooltags.app)

## Features

- **QR code scanning** - Instantly look up any spool by scanning its QR code
- **NFC tag support** - Tap your iPhone to read NFC tags on your spools (iOS only)
- **Weight tracking** - Track starting, current, and empty spool weights with low-stock alerts
- **PDF label printing** - Generate label sheets in A4 or US Letter format
- **iCloud sync** - Your spool library syncs across all your Apple devices
- **Organized library** - Group spools by manufacturer, material, variant, and color

## How it works

1. **Print labels** - Generate QR code labels from the app
2. **Tag your spools** - Stick a label on each spool or bag
3. **Scan and track** - Scan to create or update spool records, log weights after each print
