Simple to get started

Three easy steps to organize your filament collection.

1

Print labels

Generate a sheet of QR code labels from the app. Choose A4 or US Letter size, then print on regular paper or adhesive labels.

2

Tag your spools

Stick a label on each spool or the bag it came in. Each label has a unique code and human-readable slug like "brisk-wolf-0427".

3

Scan and track

Scan a label to create or update a spool record. Log the weight after each print to always know how much filament is left.

The daily workflow

  • Pick a spool for your print job
  • Scan the QR code to view current weight
  • After printing, weigh the spool and update
  • Get notified when running low on stock
